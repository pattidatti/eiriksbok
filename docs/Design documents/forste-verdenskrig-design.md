## 2. The Soul (Vision)
*   **Narrative Arc:** "The collapse of the old world order. We begin by setting the stage with the preceding century of geopolitical shifts. From the spark in Sarajevo to the industrialized slaughter of the trenches, we trace the birth of the modern era through blood and iron. Focus on the human cost of technological advancement."
*   **Visual Theme (Prompts Only):** "Autochrome color palette (grainy, soft colors), desaturated trench mud, stark contrast between aristocratic grandeur and industrial warfare. Cinematic, somber, detailed."
*   **Pedagogical Pillars:**
    1.  **Systemic Causes:** The MAIN acronym (Militarism, Alliances, Imperialism, Nationalism). *Note: Ensure links to deep-dive articles in Samfunnsfag.*
    2.  **The Human Experience:** Life in the trenches, shell shock, the christmas truce.
    3.  **Total War:** Impact on civilians, economy, and society.
    4.  **Consequences:** The redrawing of maps (partition of Ottoman Empire/Sykes-Picot), the Creation of the League of Nations (mandates), and the seeds of WWII.
