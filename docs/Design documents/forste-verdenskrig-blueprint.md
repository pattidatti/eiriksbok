## 2. The Narrative Arc
> *The Suicide of a Civilization*

**The Hook:**
"The war that was supposed to end by Christmas ended up burying a generation in mud."

**The Arc:**
We start with the naive enthusiasm of 1914 ("The Adventure"), descend into the mechanical dehumanization of the trenches (1915-1917), and conclude with the shattered, cynical world of 1918 that set the stage for total collapse in 1939.
